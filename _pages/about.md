I am an incoming assistant professor in [EECS at the University of Tennessee, Knoxville](https://www.eecs.utk.edu).
I am wrapping up my Ph.D. in [ECE at Purdue](https://engineering.purdue.edu/ECE), advised by Prof. [Jing Gao](https://engineering.purdue.edu/~jinggao/).

My research focuses on **knowledge-centric AI**.
I am broadly interested in building efficient, reliable, and adaptive AI systems that can acquire and integrate knowledge to better understand the world ([ACL'25](https://arxiv.org/abs/2502.10993), [ICML'25](https://arxiv.org/abs/2502.00602), [ICLR'25](https://arxiv.org/abs/2503.00306)), human needs ([ECCV'26](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.00407), [ICML'26](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.01511), [ACL'26](https://arxiv.org/abs/2510.07743)), and societal contexts ([ICML'24](https://arxiv.org/abs/2406.00548), [ICLR'24](https://arxiv.org/abs/2309.16487), [AAAI'23](https://arxiv.org/abs/2302.09683)) with minimal supervision.
